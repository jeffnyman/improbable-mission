Every game has a map.
That map has a layout.
The layout is made up of a series of rooms.
Those rooms are distributed over a series of levels.

There are 32 rooms in a given game.
There are 9 vertical columns where those can be.
There are 6 horizontal rows where thsoe rooms can be.
Each row is a level.
There can be at at most 9 rooms per level.
There can be at most 6 rooms per column.
